In light of positive developments over the last few weeks, Elite Financial is reopening its offices, However, we will enforce protocols that uphold the safety and health of our team and clients in mind.

The Do's and Don'ts
When Returning To Work
The Do's

Do maitain good personal hygiene in or outside office.


Do wash your hands with soap and water.


Do cover your mouth while coughing and yawning.


Do clean your hands with alcohol-based hand sanitizer.


Do see a doctor if you are feeling sick or unwell.

The Don'ts

Don't touch coworker's belongings if not necessary.


Don't forget to wash your hands when switching tasks.


Don't have contact with live animals or raw meat.


Don't go to work if you are having symptoms such as fever or cough.


Don't touch high traffic areas with your hands.
